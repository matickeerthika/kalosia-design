Understanding Anxiety & Stress

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is the body's way of responding to perceived threats or stressful situations...

Common Symptoms of Anxiety

  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Increased heart rate
  • Difficulty concentrating

Causes of Anxiety and Stress

  • Academic Pressure
  • Workload and Responsibilities
  • Relationships

Effective Ways to Cope with Anxiety

  1. Practice Deep Breathing
  2. Engage in Physical Activity
  3. Try Mindfulness and Meditation

When to Seek Professional Help

If anxiety becomes overwhelming, seeking professional help is essential...

Final Thoughts

Recognizing symptoms, identifying triggers, and implementing coping mechanisms can significantly improve mental well-being...
