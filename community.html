Our Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Kalosia Community! We are glad to have you here. Please follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone:

  1. Be Respectful: Treat all members with kindness and respect.
  2. No Hate Speech: Hate speech or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  3. Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to mental health and well-being.
  4. Privacy Matters: Do not share personal information without consent.
  5. Seek Help: If you or someone you know is in crisis, please seek professional help.
